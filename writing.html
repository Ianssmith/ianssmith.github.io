Words

It is strange, and sad, to me how often these days
people interrupt each other and talk over one another.
I would agree that a picture is worth a thousand words,
but a word can also have a thousand meanings itself,
and if you value the words you speak as having meaning
you would listen to the words of others with equal intent to the ones you speak.

My love I speak in quiet words every day to you
whispered as I carry my loads from here to there
My shoulders ache and my thighs they tremble
But it is only a balm to my heart
for my members to share in the knowledge of it's pain
And yet my eyes they shine and my smile is unyeilding
In what we shared and will share
I remember your hands I massaged in the cold
And the tears I wiped from your eyes
How I longed for the moment I would hear your voice
Each time like coming home after a long journey
The stories you gently spoke to me late in the night
You are like tempered steel in a blazing sun
When my mind first emerges from sleep your name solitary and crisp accompanies it
When I eat my meals it is your sustenance and enjoyment I taste
In idle moments you are the tears in my eyes
When I feel the breeze on my skin in a dark calm night
When I hear the notes of our music
I'm dancing with you once again like a fool in the king's court
You will do what you must, but I will keep my promises to you
My soul, your eyes pierce me deeper than any blade
Your smile is my smile and somehow I've always known you
and time has no meaning at all
